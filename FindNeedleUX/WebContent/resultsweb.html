Filtering & navigation

  • Top searchbox — case-insensitive, all columns. Multiple words = AND match.
  • Per-column filters — type in the row under each column header. Level is a dropdown.
  • Time range — pick From/To to restrict by Time column.
  • Columns ▾ — toggle which columns are visible.
  • Drag column headers to reorder.
  • Drag the right edge of a column header to resize it.
  • Click column headers to sort.
  • Export CSV — saves the currently-visible (filtered) rows.
  • Level colors — edit in Settings → Results viewer. Applied here automatically.
  • Ctrl+F — focus searchbox. Esc — close modal / clear focus.

Column visibility, order, and page size are saved across sessions.
